You can get various nose piercings, including nostril, septum, bridge, and more unique styles like rhino or nasallang piercings, each with a range of jewelry options like studs, hoops, and rings.

Body Piercings

Other body parts that can be pierced include the upper ears, nose, lips, cheeks, nipples, navel, tongue, eyebrow and genitals. Rings, studs, dumbbells and bars are some of the different types of jewellery that can be used.
